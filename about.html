FAQ

What is SpendYourDogecoins?
SpendYourDogecoins.cf is a Dogecoin Shop Directory, created by me "DimiFW" in 2019.


How often does the site get updated?
I try to search for new stores that I can add regularly.
I do check every listed store monthly to see if the stores are actually still online.
Feel free to contact me via email or via Twitter if you find a store on the list that is offline or doesn't accept Dogecoin anymore.


I can't find Dogecoin as a payment option on a listed site
There is a big amount of stores listed that don't directly show that they accept payments via Dogecoin.
I did check all of them manually though to make sure they do in fact accept Dogecoin.
Just use the "cryptocurrency" payment option and Dogecoin should show up.
If it actually doesn't though, feel free to let me know.


How can my store/charity get added?
The best way to let me know about your store/charity accepting Dogecoin is on Twitter.
Just DM @SpendYourDoge .
I do also check my email weekly, but Twitter is checked way more often.


How can I get into the "Spotlight" section with my store?
The websites in the "Spotlight" section are just ones that i personally like a lot and want to give a little bit extra exposure.
It is not something that is "for sale".
I do try to switch the sites around every few weeks though, so maybe your store is the next one that will show up there.


What will you not list on your website?
I mainly wanna try to keep the site as "kids-friendly" as possible.
This means no explicit-adult-related-stores and no drug-related-stores (exception smoking and alcohol).
Furthermore I try to use a little bit of common sense when checking potential new stores.
I can't guarantee that all of the listed stores are actually legit, but I do my best to not list stores that obviously look like a scam.


How do you host the site?
The website is hosted for free via Github Pages and the domain is a free domain from Freenom.
